Plant Leaf Disease Detector

India is one of the largest producers of crops and grains in the world for that reason it holds its place as the spine of country's economy. Rapid increase in population led to rapid growth in demand for food. Agricultural yield in India is still low in comparison to other countries of the world. This is caused due to several factors. Climate change leads to many unforeseen diseases. Plant diseases are a major threat to food security, but their rapid identification remains difficult in many parts of India due to the lack of the necessary infrastructure. This project aims to detect the plant disease.

This system helps to detect the plant disease easily by uploading image of the leaf.
To detect the disease you can upload the image of leaf. There are various subcategories of plants available which are as follows:

  • potato
  • Apple
  • Strawberry
  • Grapes
  • Peach
  • Cherry
  • Bell pepper
  • Corn
